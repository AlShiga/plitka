Genius corporation

22.04.20

We continue to share our opinions on books from our office library. Today we have Ed Catmell and his Genius Corporation. For feedback turned to CEO “AVA digital” Nikita.

“Ed Catmell is one of the founders of the very Pixar studio, from whose animated films we have been delighted for two decades. Who better than him to write a book about the difficulties of becoming a company, creative crises, disagreements, and most importantly: how to manage these geniuses?

The answer to the question is no way. Because how to “manage” in the literal sense of the word will not work. You cannot instruct the designer: “Make me such a cool project for tomorrow, think of everyone’s jaw dropping, sit down and proceed.” With all due respect to the workers of factories and other industrial enterprises, in the creative spheres the methods of mechanized and automated production do not take root at all.
